The Rock.Scot Crew

Broadcasting from towns and cities across Central Scotland.

Andy – East Kilbride's Rock Rebel

Hailing from East Kilbride in South Lanarkshire, Andy grew up blasting Biffy Clyro anthems from his bedroom. Started pirate radio in his garage before joining Rock.Scot, bringing high-energy playlists across Inverclyde, South Lanarkshire, and North Ayrshire.

  • Favourite Scottish rock band: Biffy Clyro
  • Best gig ever: Franz Ferdinand at the Barrowland Ballroom
  • Dream venue to DJ: King Tut's Wah Wah Hut
  • Fun fact: Crowd-surfed at a Big Country tribute night and lost a shoe

Alex – Johnstone's Vinyl Viking

Born and bred in Johnstone, Alex discovered rock digging through his uncle's record collection. Spins deep cuts and local shout-outs on Rock.Scot.

  • Favourite Scottish rock band: Nazareth
  • Best gig ever: Texas at the SEC Armadillo
  • Dream venue: Barrowland Ballroom
  • Fun fact: Prized scratched vinyl of Deacon Blue from secret afterparty

Stevie – Port Glasgow's Headbanger

From Port Glasgow, Stevie grew up on AC/DC and formed a garage band before DJing. Late-night shows keep Inverclyde headbanging.

  • Favourite Scottish rock band: Big Country
  • Best gig ever: Mogwai at Òran Mór
  • Dream venue: Beacon Arts Centre, Greenock
  • Fun fact: Once high-fived Angus Young at a secret pub gig

Hamilton-born Mhairi traded pop for distortion pedals. Witty banter and killer playlists dominate the Rock.Scot airwaves.
